Quora

This website plays a major role in my career. I rectify all of my doubts and questions from this website. This is a vast community of people who were in different fields proceeding different type of jobs. So that there is always someone at least one who can solve our problems and doubts. And also we can give advice and suggestions to others too, who ask for help. This a great gift to me as a student.

Rover

This website is a great opportunity for the one who owns one or more dogs and finds difficult to take care of it during their jobs and weekends. This website connects dog sitters and dog walkers to the owners so that they can easily access to others who are interested in and also a big space for the job seekers and pet lovers.

Trello

Trello is a good option for the one who is busy with his or her jobs, dating, studies, outings, partying, family, friends, and a lot of assignments and other stuff which make them forget to do things. This is a great opportunity for these type of peoples because we all are included in this category, it helps to organize things and sort things like pending – doing – done. And also can make different boards for each task. Can label things, can make a checklist and due dates as well. The major attraction is more than one person can access the boars if they are allowed so that they can organize things together.

Webometrics

For international students, this is a great place to know each and every college in over the world. And their ratings as well. We can sort colleges according to country, regions and sorted default as the order of ratings highest to lowest. This site is also provided with the websites of the college so that we can access directly for here itself.

Ontario Colleges

This is the best website to know about colleges in Ontario province, and also can directly apply for those colleges as well. The best thing is that domestic and international student can access colleges through this site. Helps to track our application and progress of our application as well.
